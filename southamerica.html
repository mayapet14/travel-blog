Peru

Machu Picchu

Explore one of the seven wonders of the world. Located high in the beautiful Andes Mountains of Peru, this ancient Incan city is an ancient masterpiece. It is a UNESCO certified landmark, and requires tickets well in advance to get in.

Lima

A coastal city located next to the Pacific Ocean. Enjoy surfing, exploring colonial architecture, and some of the world's finest cuisine. Cuy is a regional specialty... although strange to a foreigner. If you're an urban art fanatic, you will love this city.

Chile

Easter Island

The land of the mysterious stone heads. Called, moai, these statues have fascinated the world for centuries. Enjoy deep sea diving, hiking, and exploring Rapa Nui culture.

Valparaiso

Home to Pablo Neruda, this city is bound to inspire the creative mind. Valparaiso is located on the Pacific coast and is the most colorful city in South America. Walking around here is not for the faint of heart. Stairs consume local infrastructure, but some "elevators", called "funiculars", are available for some of the steeper inclines.

Argentina

Buenos Aires

Also known as the Paris of South America, Buenos Aires combines European elegance with Latin flair. The capitol of a former world power, their exceptionalism still shines through their city. Visit former First Lady, beloved Eva Peron at Cementerio de la Recolata, indulge in the best steak in the world, or world class wine. Buenos Aires has something for everyone.

Patagonia

Although not strictly limited to Argentina, as some of the mountains pass through Chile, the Argentine Patagonia is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Prepare to be mesmerized by its dramatic landscapes, featuring towering mountains, sparkling glaciers, and pristine lakes. Explore the legendary peaks of the Andes, such as the iconic Mount Fitz Roy and Cerro Torre, which offer breathtaking views and challenging hikes.
