Clothes

Backpacker Style

Wondering what to fit in your backpack as you travel the world? A good traveler packs light, in case of emergency souvenirs. It is important to wear clothes that you are comfortable in and versatility is a plus. Here is our comprehensive list of apparel, specifically for women:

  • 2 T-Shirts- For casual and sleep
  • 1 "Going Out" Shirt- Think crop top or fancy blouse
  • 1 "Cover Up" Shirt- Such as silk button-up or cardigan
  • 1 Sweater
  • 1 Skirt, 1 Short- Or mix and match
  • 3 Pair of Pants- 1 for sleep (or shorts), 1 or 2 pants for day wear.
  • Shower Shoes- A MUST.
  • Gym Shoes/ Hiking Boots- One or the other
  • Undergarments/ Socks- Just be smart. A bar of soap and a sink is a great washer.
